Hi, and welcome!

My name is Ty, and I am a CIRGET Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics at the Université du Québec à Montréal, working with Steve Boyer and Duncan McCoy.

My research interests are in mapping class groups, ordered groups, and low-dimensional topology. See my maths page for more.

I coorganise the Big Surf(aces) seminar with Federica Fanoni and Alan McLeay.
